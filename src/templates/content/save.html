Save Wager

IOS

  1. Tap the Share button on Safari
  2. Find by slide to the right “Add to Home Screen.”
  3. Tap it, then tap Add (top right)

Android

  1. Tap Settings (Top right corner) on Chrome
  2. Slide down and tap “Add to Home Screen.”

Desktop and Mac

  1. Go to Settings (top right corner)
  2. Click Install “Wager”
