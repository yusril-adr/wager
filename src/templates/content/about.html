Wager

Wager (Wallet Manager) is an app that will help you to organize your personal cashflow. You can add or delete transaction and the app will help you calculate your income, cash and balance in one month. The app also have a growth feature that help you know how your balance in last 3 months. Wager is an progressive webs app so you can save this as an native app in desktop and phone.
